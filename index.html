Overview

Welcome to our piece of heaven located in Beach Haven Park in Long Beach Island, NJ. We are just a 5 minute walk to the beautiful life-guarded beach of 100th Street. Perfect for families, our block is also conveniently located at a light so it makes crossing over Long Beach Blvd very simple. Acme supermarket is just three blocks away. You can bike 11 blocks south to Jersey Girl Grill for breakfast outside on the patio or walk the 6 blocks north to Skipper Dipper for the best ice cream around. Mini golf, Fantasy Island Amusement Park, Thundering Surf Waterpark and multiple fantastic restaurants located a 10 minute drive south.

We have a beautiful tree lined, fenced and private backyard. Our street is a quiet, bayside block with mostly full time summer families. There are very few rentals on our block so we expect our renters to be kind, courteous and respectful to our neighbors.

Please note the home has many stairs and is not wheelchair accessible. We do not accept pets nor renters under age 25. Linens and bath towels are not provided.
